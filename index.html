Reading

I've been an avid reader ever since I was a child. I mainly enjoy reading manga and comic books. Within that, my favourite genres are horror, mystery, and stories that contain LGBTQ+ themes.

Gaming

For as long as I've had access to a computer, I've considered myself a gamer. My favourite genre of games are horror games, mystery/puzzle games, and time-management games. I look forward to creating my own games some day, and my all time favourite games include the Pokemon series and Danganronpa.

Animation

I've loved and enjoyed animation in many forms. I am an avid watcher of both North American cartoons and Japanese anime, and have watched them for most of my life. I also love the process that goes into creating animation, and in my free time I love to study the art and process of animating. Music

A newer interest of mine is a fascination and desire to learn to play string intstruments I have always loved instruments with a low, 'basey' tone that adds depth to music, and have begun to learn to play the cello. Other instruments I would love to learn are the bass guitair and the drums.
